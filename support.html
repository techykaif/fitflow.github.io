Support Center

We are here to help you with any issues or queries you might have. Check out our FAQs below or send us a message.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions to receive a reset link in your email.

How can I track my daily activities?

Our tracker allows you to log your daily activities through the "Go to Tracker" feature. You can manually input your activity data or sync with fitness devices.

Is my data secure?

Your data is stored securely with encryption, and we follow strict data privacy regulations to ensure your information is safe.
